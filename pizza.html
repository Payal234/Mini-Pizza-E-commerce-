VEG

A veg pizza is a meatless pizza topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various vegetables, baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. It's a delicious and popular choice for vegetarians and vegans.

A non-vegeterian pizza is a flavorful creation featuring toppings like pepperoni,sausage, ham,or chicken along with cheese and a zesty tomato sauce , all baked on a crispy crust ,offering a hearty and meaty indulgence.
